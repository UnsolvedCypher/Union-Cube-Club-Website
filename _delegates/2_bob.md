description: Bob Burton is one of the most famous speedcubers in the United States. He first started speedcubing in 2001, and was well known for his record-breaking Magic, Master Magic, and Clock times. He also started cubewhiz.com where he posts resources for beginners and advanced cubers for various puzzles. Bob teaches math in the Bronx, New York, and is the Senior Delegate for the Eastern US and Canada. Bob was married at the Liberty Science Center, the venue of US Nationals 2014, on Pi Day of 2015 (3-14-15).
