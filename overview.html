Yearly Overview of Competitions

This page contains a list of competitions that usually happen in the Union area every year. Although this list will be kept as accurate as possible, no competitions are guaranteed until they are announced on CubingUSA. As competitions are announced, they will be linked to from here, and dates will be added here as they become available.

Competitions for the year have finished - check back later for more info on next year's competitions!
